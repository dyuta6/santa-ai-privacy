# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** 18/11/2025

Welcome to **Santa AI - Photo Editor** ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience while using our mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

We may collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

- **Device Information:** We collect a unique device identifier to manage your account and provide personalized services.
- **Photos:** When you use our photo editing features, you may select photos from your device. These photos are processed locally on your device and are not stored on our servers unless you explicitly choose to save them.
- **Subscription Information:** If you purchase a subscription, we collect transaction information through our payment processor, RevenueCat, and Apple App Store or Google Play Store.

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use our App, we automatically collect certain information, including:

- **Usage Data:** Information about how you interact with the App, such as features used and time spent in the App.
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
- **App Performance Data:** Crash reports and performance metrics to help us improve the App.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide, maintain, and improve our services
- Process transactions and manage subscriptions
- Send you technical notices, updates, and support messages
- Respond to your comments, questions, and requests
- Monitor and analyze usage patterns and trends
- Detect, prevent, and address technical issues
- Personalize your experience within the App

## 3. Third-Party Services

### 3.1 Firebase

We use Google Firebase to store and manage user data, including device identifiers and subscription status. Firebase's privacy practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy. For more information, please visit: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### 3.2 RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions and purchases. RevenueCat processes payment information and subscription data. For more information about RevenueCat's privacy practices, please visit: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

### 3.3 Apple App Store & Google Play Store

When you make in-app purchases, your payment information is processed by Apple (for iOS) or Google (for Android) through their respective app stores. We do not have access to your payment card details. Please review Apple's and Google's privacy policies for more information.

## 4. Photo Processing and Storage

**Important:** All photo editing and processing occurs locally on your device. We do not upload your photos to our servers unless you explicitly choose to save or share them through our App. Photos you select for editing remain on your device and are not transmitted to our servers during the editing process.

## 5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. When you delete your account or request deletion of your data, we will delete or anonymize your information in accordance with applicable laws.

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:
